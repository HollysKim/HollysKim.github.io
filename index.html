Project Abstract:

Centered around transporation and urban planning, my project looks at NYC's motor vehicular collison accident statistics over the years in order to discover any plausible correlations and relationships between data variables such as boroughs, time of day and week, etc. In a city as populous and fast-paced as New York City, the data sets I'm using relating to motor vehicular collisions is massive in size. Thus, data science tools such as Pandas and SQL lend a significant helping hand in cleaning and filtering all the data collected over the years. By the end, I hope to uncover any plausible trends beneficial to fighting New York City's long-staying vehicular accident plague.
